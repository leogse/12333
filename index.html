About Eve RPG

Humans have built their kingdoms through centuries of peaceful efforts, while maintaining a safe border from the dark magical forces and monsters that lurk beyond civilization. Sam, a young hunter, lives in a remote cabin deep in the forest. When Sam's home is invaded by a horde of trolls and orcs, he must escape with his own life. Sam's journey to his new home begins, and what he doesn't know is that a greater threat looms behind him, and the survival of the entire kingdom will depend on whether he becomes the hero he was born to be.

Gameplay

Horuma has a unique world-switching mechanic. A World Warp ability turns the special in-game areas 180 degrees, transfers the protagonist into another dimension and unlocks new locations.

Combat

The core element of the Horuma combat system are various combos. The hero can fight enemies on the ground, toss them up and continue to battle in mid-air. Equipped with a quarterstaff and a hand fan Mako can perform melee and ranged style attacks.
